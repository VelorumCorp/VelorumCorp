Who we are

Originally founded in 2012, Velorum Corporation N.V. has received and perpetually maintained authorization and license by Antillephone N.V. to conduct remote gaming activities. Such activities include promoting, managing, supporting and operating online businesses in this field which requires in-depth knowledge of products, content, management and technology.

Throughout the years Velorum Corporation N.V. developed a sound foundation to run its business with the help of its suppliers, partners, affiliates and most importantly its team.
